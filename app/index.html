What does work-life
wellness even mean?

  • The History of WORK-LIFE BALANCE

    The term ‘work-life balance’ was coined over 50 years ago. With the amount that has changed since then in both work and life, we’re overdue for updated terminology.

  • The Emergence of WORK-LIFE INTEGRATION

    Rather than compartmentalizing and dividing time equally between work and life, we should fuel people as whole beings both in and out of the workplace.

  • The Need for WORK-LIFE WELLNESS

    Work-life wellness is a state of mind-body wellbeing found at the intersection of life and work. Now more than ever, leaders and businesses must anticipate what's to come in this space.
